Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Game Library ("we," "our," or "the service") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen unique identifier
  • Display Name: Your preferred name for display purposes
  • Password: Stored as a secure hash (we never store your actual password)
  • User Role: Whether you're a regular user or contributor
  • Account Creation Date: When your account was created
  • Last Update Date: When your profile was last modified

2.2 Google OAuth Information

If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Google ID: Your unique Google identifier
  • Email Address: From your Google account

2.3 Contributor Content

Contributors may upload game-related information and images, which we process and store to display in our game library, including:

  • Game Information: Titles, developer names, release dates, summaries, websites, genres, platforms
  • Game Media: Cover images and screenshots

2.4 Technical Information

  • Authentication Tokens: JWT tokens for maintaining your login session
  • Local Storage Data: Site preferences like theme settings (light/dark mode)
  • System Logs: Technical logs for service operation and debugging

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • User authentication and authorization
  • Maintaining your user account and preferences
  • Providing access to the Game Library application
  • Displaying contributor-uploaded game information and media in our game library
  • Technical service operation and maintenance
  • Debugging and troubleshooting service issues

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to any third parties.

The only exception is Google OAuth, which is used solely for authentication purposes when you choose to sign in with Google. This interaction is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms and never stored in plain text
  • JWT tokens are signed with private keys for authentication
  • Database access is controlled and monitored
  • System logs are securely managed

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information:

  • Until you delete your account
  • Until you request data deletion
  • As long as your account remains active

System logs may be retained for technical and security purposes for a reasonable period.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what personal data we have about you
  • Update: Modify your profile information through the service
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Export: Request export of your personal data by contacting outofstack.inqueries@outlook.com

8. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Store authentication tokens for your login session
  • Save your site preferences (such as theme selection)

We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

9. International Data Transfers

This service may be accessed from anywhere in the world. By using the service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this privacy policy, regardless of your location.

10. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of the service after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: When you create an account or use Google OAuth
  • Legitimate Interests: For service operation, security, and technical maintenance
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the authentication service you've requested
